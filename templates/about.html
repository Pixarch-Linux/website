Welcome to Pixarch!

A minimalist Linux distribution designed for developers, creators, and tech enthusiasts who value performance, aesthetics, and freedom.

Our Mission

To create a Linux distribution that combines the raw power of Arch Linux with a beautifully crafted, user-friendly experience. We believe in giving users complete control without sacrificing simplicity.

Design Philosophy

Minimalism meets functionality. Every pixel, every configuration is carefully considered to provide a seamless, distraction-free computing environment that looks as good as it performs.

Open Source Commitment

We're dedicated to the open-source ecosystem. Pixarch is built on transparency, community collaboration, and the belief that software should be free, powerful, and accessible to everyone.
